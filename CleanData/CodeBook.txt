------------------------------
NOTES
------------------------------

There are no units of measurements as they are all normalized and bounded within [-1,1]
 
Each signal is also noted to be in the time or frequency domail. Signals were measured in the time domain.  Fast Fourier Transforms were used to convert 
from the time domain to frequency domain.

All columns except the "Activity" column has int type data.  The activity column's data type is char.

------------------------------
COLUMN NAMES EXPLANATION

Subject:  
Thirty people were tracked and labeled with a number.

Activity:
Each subject performed 6 activities: Laying, Sitting, Standing, Walking, Walking Downstairs, and Walking Upstairs.  Each activity was performed several times.
